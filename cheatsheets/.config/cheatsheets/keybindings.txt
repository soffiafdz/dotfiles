             Feels like "Hyper" - same physical position everywhere

MOVE MODE (Aerospace) / SHIFT MODIFIER (dwm)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Aerospace: Press Hyper + M, then:
  Move window           H/J/K/L
  Send to workspace     1-9
  Cancel                Esc

dwm: Use Super + Shift + key:
  Send to tag           Super + Shift + 1-9
  Send to monitor       Super + Shift + ,/.
  Push in stack         Super + Shift + J/K

DWM-SPECIFIC
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Toggle bar            Super + Shift + B

  Screenshot            Super + P (flameshot)
  Full screenshot       Super + Shift + P
  Mount drive           Super + M
  Unmount drive         Super + Shift + M
  Volume down           Super + -
  Volume up             Super + Shift + -
  Mute                  Super + =
  Gaps toggle           Super + S
  Gaps reset            Super + Shift + S
